47inf.org
Site Administrator & Developer / 2016 - 2018
The 47inf.org website is a legacy website that was originally designed to assist veterans of the 47th Infantry during the Vietnam War connect with one another and keep in touch, as well as register for their Biennial Regimental Reunion. From 2016 to 2018 various site updates were implemented and new systems were developed to work with both existing database models and new database schemas.

Registrants saw the addition of several features, such as improved account registration and management systems, online reunion registration, online payment processing, reunion registration modification features(add a guest/event post registration), receipt record systems, and attendee rosters.

New account administration software, admin tools, report generation tools, quick registration tools, and mass-email system tools were developed for administration personnel.
